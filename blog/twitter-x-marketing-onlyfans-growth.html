Understanding the 2025 Twitter/X Algorithm

The Twitter algorithm underwent massive changes in 2023-2025 under X ownership. Understanding these changes is critical for OnlyFans marketing success.

What the Algorithm Prioritizes (Ranked by Impact)

  1. Engagement Velocity (40% of ranking weight): How quickly your tweet gets likes, retweets, and replies in the first 15-60 minutes determines viral potential
  2. Engagement Quality (25%): Interactions from verified accounts and accounts with high follower counts carry more algorithmic weight
  3. Media Content (20%): Tweets with images/videos get 3-5x more impressions than text-only tweets
  4. Engagement Rate (10%): Your historical engagement rate affects how widely your new tweets are distributed
  5. Recency (5%): Fresh content gets prioritized, but viral tweets can stay in "For You" feeds for 24-48 hours

What Kills Your Reach

  • External Links (Especially OnlyFans Links): Tweets with outbound links get 50-70% lower reach. The algorithm wants to keep users on platform.
  • Shadowban Triggers: Certain words, excessive hashtags, and spam behavior tank your visibility
  • Low Engagement History: If your recent tweets consistently underperform, the algorithm shows your new tweets to fewer people
  • Posting at Wrong Times: Tweeting when your audience isn't active means low initial engagement velocity, killing viral potential

‚ö° Critical Algorithm Insight:

The algorithm makes a "viral decision" in the first 15-60 minutes after posting. If your tweet gets strong engagement velocity during this window, it gets pushed to "For You" feeds of users who don't follow you. If engagement is weak, it dies in obscurity.

Implication: Strategic engagement in the first hour after posting is more important than total follower count.

Shadowban Prevention and Recovery

Twitter shadowbans devastate reach without warning. Your tweets simply stop showing up in feeds and search results, killing growth overnight.

Common Shadowban Triggers

  1. OnlyFans Links in Tweets: Direct OnlyFans links get heavily suppressed or trigger instant shadowban
  2. Banned Keywords: Certain explicit words tank visibility (even for adult content)
  3. Excessive Hashtags: More than 2-3 hashtags per tweet looks like spam
  4. Repetitive Content: Posting the same tweet multiple times triggers spam detection
  5. Mass Follow/Unfollow: Following 100+ accounts per day or aggressive unfollowing
  6. Too Many External Links: Multiple tweets with links in short timeframe

Shadowban Prevention Protocol

‚úÖ DO:

  • Use "link in bio" or "check pinned tweet" instead of direct OnlyFans links
  • Put OnlyFans links in pinned replies to promo tweets (NOT in main tweet)
  • Vary your tweet content and wording significantly
  • Use strategic censoring in images (blur or emoji over explicit areas)
  • Limit hashtags to 1-2 per tweet maximum
  • Keep your engagement rate high (proves you're not spam)

‚ùå DON'T:

  • Post OnlyFans.com links directly in tweets
  • Use explicit language that triggers filters (test alternatives: "spicy" instead of explicit words)
  • Copy-paste the same promotional tweet daily
  • Follow more than 50 accounts per day
  • Use automated posting tools that Twitter hasn't approved

How to Check if You're Shadowbanned

  1. Log out of Twitter and search for your exact username
  2. Check if your profile appears in search results
  3. Ask a friend (who doesn't follow you) to search for your recent tweets by keyword
  4. Use shadowban checker tools (search "Twitter shadowban test")

Shadowban Recovery Strategy

If you are shadowbanned:

  1. Stop posting for 48-72 hours (let the system reset)
  2. Delete recent tweets with links or explicit content
  3. Submit a support ticket to Twitter/X (often resolves automatically)
  4. Resume posting with 100% clean content (no links, no borderline language)
  5. Rebuild engagement rate through pods and reply game
  6. Wait 7-14 days for shadowban to fully lift
