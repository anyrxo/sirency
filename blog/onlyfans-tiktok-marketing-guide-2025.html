TikTok Algorithm Mastery: Getting Your Content on the FYP

Understanding TikTok's recommendation algorithm is essential for reaching the massive audience needed to drive significant OnlyFans subscriptions. Here's what matters in 2025:

The TikTok Algorithm Ranking Factors

1. Watch Time & Completion Rate (Highest Weight)

TikTok prioritizes videos that viewers watch to completion. Strategies to maximize completion rate:

  • Hook viewers in the first 1-2 seconds with movement or intrigue
  • Keep videos under 15 seconds when possible (higher completion rate)
  • Use pattern interrupts (scene changes, sound switches) every 3-4 seconds
  • End with a question or CTA that encourages profile visits

2. Engagement Rate (Likes, Comments, Shares)

Videos that generate high engagement get amplified. Boost engagement by:

  • Asking questions in captions to encourage comments
  • Creating "controversial" content that sparks discussion (stay within guidelines)
  • Using trending sounds that viewers recognize and engage with
  • Responding to all comments in the first 1-2 hours (signals high engagement)

3. Video Information (Sounds, Hashtags, Captions)

TikTok analyzes video metadata to understand content and audience fit:

  • Sounds: Use trending sounds (check Creative Center daily)
  • Hashtags: Mix of broad (#fyp, #foryou) and niche-specific tags
  • Captions: Descriptive but concise, include keywords naturally

4. Account Activity & Consistency

Active accounts that post consistently receive algorithmic favor:

  • Post 3-5 times daily at optimal times
  • Maintain regular posting schedule (algorithm learns your pattern)
  • Engage with other creators' content (watch, like, comment)
  • Use TikTok features (stitches, duets, effects) to signal active participation

The "Hook-Retain-Convert" Video Formula
