Category 2: Monetization Features (The Revenue Boosters)

Feature #5: Campaigns (The Subscriber Acquisition Weapon)

What it is: OnlyFans promotional links with tracking and customization

Campaign Types:

1. Trial Links (Free Trial Campaigns)

  • What it does: Gives subscribers X days free access, then auto-charges subscription price
  • Best for: Converting cold traffic (Reddit, Twitter) who won't pay upfront
  • Strategy: Set 3-7 day trial, limit to 100-500 uses, expire after 30 days
  • Conversion data: 3-day trials convert 8-15% to paying after trial ends (vs. 2-4% for full-price links)

2. Discount Links (Percentage-Off Campaigns)

  • What it does: Offers X% off first month subscription
  • Best for: Limited-time promotions, re-activation of churned subscribers
  • Strategy: 30-50% off, 7-14 day expiration to create urgency
  • Example: "First 100 subscribers get 50% off (normally $19.99, now $9.99). Link expires in 48 hours!"

3. Tracked Links (No Discount, Just Analytics)

  • What it does: Tracks which traffic source drives most subscribers
  • Best for: Measuring ROI of different platforms
  • Strategy: Create separate link for Instagram, Twitter, Reddit, TikTok ‚Üí track conversions ‚Üí allocate effort to best-performing

Campaign Strategy: The Funnel Stack

Traffic Source Hierarchy:

  • Instagram/TikTok (High-intent traffic): Full-price link or small discount (10-20% off)
  • Twitter (Medium-intent): 30-40% discount or 3-day trial
  • Reddit (Low-intent, price-sensitive): 7-day trial or 50% off

Why this works: Match offer aggressiveness to traffic quality. Don't discount for high-quality traffic that would pay full price.

Feature #6: Promotions (The Limited-Time Revenue Spike)

What it is: Temporary subscription price reductions visible on your profile

How it differs from Campaigns: Promotions change your listed price publicly. Campaigns are trackable links with custom terms.

When to Use Promotions:

  • Re-subscribe campaigns: Win back churned subscribers (50% off to return)
  • Seasonal sales: Black Friday, New Year, Valentine's Day (capitalize on spending mood)
  • Subscriber milestones: "Hit 1,000 subs! Celebrating with 40% off for next 100 people"
  • Dead periods: January, September (post-holiday lulls) - run promotion to maintain revenue

Promotion Best Practices:

  • Duration: 24-72 hours max (urgency disappears after 3 days)
  • Discount: 30-60% off (must feel significant)
  • Communication: Announce across all platforms (IG, Twitter, OnlyFans) simultaneously
  • Frequency: Max 1-2x per month (too frequent = people wait for sales instead of paying full price)
