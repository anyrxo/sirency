3. Content Strategy That Prevents the $3k Plateau

There's a reason 90% of creators get stuck at $2k-4k/month—they're randomly posting content without understanding subscriber psychology.

Elite agencies implement the 30/30/30/10 content framework:

  • 30% - Tease & Preview content (free/low-cost content designed to attract and retain subscribers)
  • 30% - Core subscription value (regular feed content that justifies monthly subscription)
  • 30% - Premium PPV content (high-value content sold separately to maximize revenue per subscriber)
  • 10% - Custom & personalized content (VIP experiences, custom videos, personal interactions)

This distribution keeps subscribers engaged, prevents churn, and maximizes average revenue per user (ARPU). Solo creators typically post 90% free content and wonder why they can't monetize.
