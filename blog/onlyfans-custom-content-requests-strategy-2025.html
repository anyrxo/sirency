Understanding OnlyFans Custom Content in 2025

What Is Custom Content?

Custom content is personalized material created specifically for an individual subscriber based on their unique requests, preferences, or fantasies. Unlike standard content visible to all subscribers or even PPV content sent to many people, custom content is:

  • One-of-a-kind: Created exclusively for one person based on their specific request
  • Personalized: Often includes the subscriber's name, specific outfits, scenarios, or fetishes
  • Premium-priced: Commands 3-10x higher pricing than standard content due to exclusivity and effort
  • Private: Delivered only to the requester (though you may reuse with permission or after exclusivity period)

Why Custom Content Is So Profitable

  • Premium pricing power: Charge $100-500+ per request (vs $20-50 for standard PPV)
  • No distribution limits: One piece of content = one high payment (not split among many buyers)
  • Superfan magnet: Custom content buyers are your highest-value subscribers who spend repeatedly
  • Predictable revenue: Build a waiting list for customs creating guaranteed weekly income
  • Relationship building: Custom content creates deep personal connections, increasing retention
  • Upsell opportunities: Custom buyers often purchase additional PPV and tip regularly

Custom Content Workflow & Management

Step 1: Initial Request & Screening

When a subscriber requests custom content:

  1. Have them describe in detail: Exactly what they want (outfit, scenario, length, specific requirements)
  2. Screen against your boundaries: Does this request fit within your comfort zone?
  3. Assess complexity: Simple or complex? What premium factors apply?
  4. Check their history: Are they a good subscriber? Any red flags?

Step 2: Quote & Payment

  1. Provide detailed quote: "This would be $150 for a 10-minute video with outfit change and name-saying"
  2. Explain pricing: Break down why it costs what it does (base + time + complexity)
  3. Set expectations: Turnaround time, delivery method, exclusivity terms
  4. Require 100% upfront payment: Never create content before receiving full payment
  5. Confirm details in writing: "Once you send $150, I'll create [exact description]"

Step 3: Production Scheduling

  • Batch similar customs: Shoot multiple customs on same day if they use similar setups/outfits
  • Set dedicated custom days: E.g., "I fulfill customs every Wednesday and Saturday"
  • Manage waiting list: If demand exceeds capacity, create waitlist with expected fulfillment dates
  • Rush orders: Charge significant premium (+50-100%) for 24-48 hour turnaround

Step 4: Creation & Quality Control

  • Follow the request exactly: Reread their specifications before shooting
  • Film extra footage: Better to have too much than realize you're short after the fact
  • Check quality: Lighting, audio, framing‚Äîcustom content should be high quality
  • Review before sending: Ensure you delivered exactly what was requested

Step 5: Delivery & Follow-Up

  • Deliver via OnlyFans DM: Send as locked content or unlocked depending on agreement
  • Include personal message: "Hope you enjoy this! Let me know what you think üòò"
  • Watermark if non-exclusive: Protect your content from piracy
  • Ask for feedback: "Did I capture what you were looking for?"
  • Upsell next custom: "If you ever want another custom, just let me know!"

Legal, Safety & Consent Considerations

Content Rights & Ownership

  • Default: You retain all rights to content you create, even if custom commissioned
  • Personal use only: Specify that custom content is for buyer's personal use only (not redistribution)
  • Exclusivity terms: Define whether/when you can resell the content
  • Watermarking: Watermark non-exclusive customs to prevent piracy

Privacy & Anonymity

  • Never share personal information: Real name, address, phone number, social media
  • Face policy: Decide if you show face in customs (charge premium if yes)
  • Identifiable details: Be cautious about showing unique tattoos, backgrounds, etc.
  • Metadata removal: Strip EXIF data from photos/videos before sending

Consent & Boundaries

  • You can refuse any request: No explanation needed if something makes you uncomfortable
  • You can stop mid-production: If a request feels wrong during creation, refund and cancel
  • Clear communication: If unsure about a request, ask clarifying questions before committing
  • Revisions policy: Limit revisions to prevent scope creep (e.g., "one round of minor revisions included")

Common Custom Content Mistakes to Avoid

1. Underpricing Custom Content

New creators often charge $30-50 for customs worth $150+. Remember: custom content is personalized, exclusive, and requires 1-on-1 communication. Charge accordingly.

2. Creating Content Before Receiving Payment

Never create customs before receiving 100% upfront payment. You have zero leverage after content is created. Scammers will promise to pay after delivery‚Äîdon't fall for it.

3. No Clear Boundaries or Menu

Without defined boundaries and pricing, you'll constantly negotiate and field inappropriate requests. Create a clear menu and boundaries document from day one.

4. Over-Promising on Turnaround Time

Promising 24-hour delivery then taking 5 days damages trust. Set realistic timelines (5-7 days) and deliver early to exceed expectations.

5. Unlimited Revisions

Subscribers will request endless changes if allowed. Limit to "one round of minor revisions" or charge for additional revision rounds.

6. Not Batching Production

Creating one custom at a time is inefficient. Batch similar requests together‚Äîfilm multiple customs on same day with similar setups.

7. Giving Away Exclusivity for Free

If a subscriber wants true exclusivity (you never resell), charge 2-3x normal price. Don't give away this valuable right without compensation.
