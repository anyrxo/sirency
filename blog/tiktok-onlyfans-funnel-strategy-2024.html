TikTok Algorithm Optimization

Understanding how TikTok decides what goes viral is critical for growth.

What the Algorithm Prioritizes

  1. Watch Time / Completion Rate (50% of ranking): Videos watched to completion get massively boosted
  2. Re-watches (20%): If people watch your video multiple times, it signals high quality
  3. Engagement Rate (15%): Likes, comments, shares drive reach
  4. Share Rate (10%): Videos people share to friends get huge algorithmic boost
  5. Follows from Video (5%): Videos that convert viewers to followers rank higher

Algorithm Optimization Tactics

Tactic #1: Hook in First 1 Second

90% of viewers decide to scroll or watch in the first second. Your hook must be immediate.

  • ‚úÖ Start mid-action (already talking, moving)
