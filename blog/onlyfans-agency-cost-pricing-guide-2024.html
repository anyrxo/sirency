Model 2: Tiered Commission (Performance-Based)

How it works: OnlyFans agency percentage decreases as your earnings increase.

Cons:

  • Higher initial commission for new creators
  • Complex tracking if tier changes monthly

Best for: Creators expecting rapid growth from $5K to $50K+/month

Typical agencies: Premium OnlyFans marketing agencies focused on scaling top performers

Model 3: Flat Monthly Fee

How it works: OnlyFans agency cost is a fixed monthly amount regardless of earnings.

Typical range: $500-$5,000/month

Example: You pay $2,000/month for full agency services (marketing, chatting, content strategy)

Pros:

  • Predictable cost (no surprise high commissions)

  • No percentage taken from your earnings

Cons:

  • Upfront financial risk (pay even if you earn $0)
  • Expensive for new/low-earning creators
  • Agency has less incentive to maximize your earnings

Best for: Established creators earning $20K+/month who want to keep larger % of revenue

Typical agencies: Boutique OnlyFans model agencies working with high-tier creators
