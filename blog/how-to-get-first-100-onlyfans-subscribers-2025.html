Advanced Tactics for Faster Growth

Tactic 1: Free Trial Links

Free trial links (FTLs) are controversial but effective. They allow users to subscribe for free for a set period (usually 3-7 days) before being charged.

When to use FTLs:

  • On Reddit (high-intent traffic, low conversion resistance)
  • For cold traffic who doesn't know you yet
  • When you need to boost subscriber count quickly for social proof

How to make FTLs profitable:

  • Send PPV messages during the trial period (many will purchase)
  • Offer "trial subscriber exclusive" PPV bundles
  • 35-50% of trial users will convert to paid if you engage them properly

Tactic 2: Collaboration Content

Partner with other creators at your level to cross-promote:

  • Create collab content and post on both accounts
  • Do shoutout exchanges on Instagram Stories
  • Mention each other in Reddit posts
  • Each collaboration exposes you to an entirely new audience of potential subscribers

Tactic 3: Giveaway Campaigns

Run Instagram giveaways to explode your following:

  • "Follow me + tag 2 friends to win a free month subscription"
  • Partner with 2-3 other creators for joint giveaway to multiply reach
  • Giveaways can add 500-1,000 Instagram followers in a week

Common Mistakes That Kill Growth

  • Promoting an empty page: Never send traffic before you have 15+ posts live

  • Ignoring engagement: Not replying to comments/DMs makes people feel ignored
  • Pricing too low: $3.99 pages attract terrible subscribers who never tip or buy PPV
  • Only posting explicit content: Personality and lifestyle content builds connection
  • Giving up too early: Most creators quit right before their growth explodes
