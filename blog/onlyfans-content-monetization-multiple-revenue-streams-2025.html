Custom Content Request Management

1. Create Custom Content Menu

Publish clear pricing and options to reduce negotiation time:

  • Photo packages: 10 photos ($75), 20 photos ($125), 50 photos ($250)
  • Video packages: 5 minutes ($150), 10 minutes ($250), 20 minutes ($400)
  • Add-ons: Name personalization (+$50), specific outfit (+$25), fetish content (+$100)
  • Turnaround time: Standard (7 days), Rush (3 days, +50%), Express (24 hours, +100%)

2. Custom Request Workflow

  • Initial inquiry: Subscriber describes custom request
  • Quote & scope: Creator provides price, timeline, and deliverable details
  • Payment upfront: Require full payment before creating content (use PPV message for payment)
  • Creation: Produce custom content within promised timeframe
  • Delivery: Send via PPV message (even though paid, for record-keeping)
  • Follow-up: Check satisfaction; potential for repeat custom orders

3. Custom Content Boundaries

Establish clear boundaries to protect yourself and maintain professionalism:

  • No illegal content: Refuse anything illegal, dangerous, or against platform TOS
  • No personal information sharing: Never include real name, location, identifying details
  • No face showing (if policy): Maintain boundaries around identity protection
  • Minimum pricing: Never accept custom requests below your established minimums
  • Revision limits: Include "1 revision included" to prevent endless changes
  • Refund policy: "All custom content sales final" (no refunds once delivered)

Scaling Custom Content Revenue

Strategy 1: Repurpose Custom Content

  • If contract allows, sell "exclusive for 30 days, then added to vault"
  • Charge premium for true exclusivity, lower price for temporary exclusivity
  • Remove personalization (names) and resell as general PPV content

Strategy 2: Template Custom Content

  • Create 5-10 "template" customs in advance (various scenarios/outfits)
  • When custom request matches template, deliver quickly and charge full custom price
  • Reduce production time by 80% while maintaining custom pricing

Strategy 3: Batch Custom Production

  • Accept custom requests throughout the week
  • Dedicate one production day to filming all customs at once
  • Efficiency of single setup reduces time per custom by 50-70%
